Step 1

* Input: Four numbers `[a, b, c, d]`
* Output: A sum of two numbers that will serve as a multiplier base
* Execution: Choose two numbers and compute their sum: `sum = a + b` or `sum = b + a`.

Step 2

* Input: Sum from Step 1 and a third number
* Output: Multiply sum by the third number
* Execution: `product = sum * c`.

Step 3

* Input: Product from Step 2 and the remaining number
* Output: Add or subtract the remaining number to reach 24
* Execution: Check if `product + d == 24` or `product - d == 24`.

Step 4

* Input: Result from Step 3
* Output: Final verification
* Execution: If result equals 24 → success. If not, try different pairings or operation orders in Step 1 and Step 2.
