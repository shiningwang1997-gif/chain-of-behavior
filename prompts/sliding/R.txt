Step 1
- Input: Current board `Board` and the position of the empty space `0`.
- Output: Updated board `Board_updated` after moving empty space right by one cell, and recorded action `action = "R"`.
- Execution: 
  1. Identify the current row and column of the empty space `(r0, c0)`.
  2. Compute the new column after moving right: `c1 = c0 + 1`.
     - Ensure `c1` does not exceed the board boundary.
  3. Swap the empty space `0` with the element at `(r0, c1)` in the board.
  4. Record the action `"R"` in the moves list.
  5. Return the updated board `Board_updated` and the action `"R"`.
