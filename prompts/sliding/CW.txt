* **Input:**
  Current board `board`, rectangle `[r0, r1, c0, c1]`.

* **Output:**
  Move direction of `0` as `"U" | "D" | "L" | "R"` and the updated board after one clockwise rotation.

* **Execution:**

  1. Extract the outer-layer coordinates of the rectangle `[r0, r1, c0, c1]` in clockwise order.
  2. Collect the values of these coordinates into a list `V`.
  3. Perform one clockwise cyclic shift:
     `V' = [V[-1]] + V[:-1]`.
  4. Write back `V'` to the same outer-layer coordinates in order.
  5. Locate the position of `0` before and after rotation.
  6. Convert the coordinate change of `0` to move direction:
     `(x, y) → (x-1, y) = "U"`,
     `(x, y) → (x+1, y) = "D"`,
     `(x, y) → (x, y-1) = "L"`,
     `(x, y) → (x, y+1) = "R"`.
  7. Output the move and the updated board.
