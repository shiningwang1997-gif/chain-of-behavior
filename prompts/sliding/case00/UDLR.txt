**Step 1**

* **Input:** Board, target number 1, target position `(0,0)`, empty space at `(1,1)`, and number 1 at `(2,2)`.
* **Output:** Current board state after one arbitrary move and the move direction.
* **Execution:**

  1. **Apply a free displacement move:**

     * Move the empty space **one step in any valid direction**:

       * `"U"`, `"D"`, `"L"`, or `"R"`.
     * This move is only used to **break the dead center configuration** and prepare for normal rectangle rotation in the next step.

  2. **Output:**

     ```json
     {
       "moves": ["U"] or ["D"] or ["L"] or ["R"],
       "new_board": updated_board
     }
     ```
