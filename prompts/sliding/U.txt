## **Step 1**

* **Input:** Current board `Board` and the position of the empty space `0`.
* **Output:** Updated board `Board_updated` after moving the empty space **up** by one cell, and recorded action `action = "U"`.
* **Execution:**

  1. Identify the current row and column of the empty space `(r0, c0)`.
  2. Compute the new row after moving up:

     ```
     r1 = r0 - 1
     ```

     * Ensure `r1` is not less than `0` (upper boundary).
  3. Swap the empty space `0` with the element at `(r1, c0)` in the board.
  4. Record the action `"U"` in the moves list.
  5. Return the updated board `Board_updated` and the action `"U"`.
